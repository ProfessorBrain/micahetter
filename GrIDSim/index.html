Welcome to GrID

A browser version of the GrID anatomy card activity: classify GI features into foregut, midgut, and hindgut regions while keeping every Sudoku unit balanced.

How to play

  1. Choose a card from the card bank, then choose a cell on the board.
  2. Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain exactly three foregut, three midgut, and three hindgut cards.
  3. A completed 3x3 box receives a green rim when it contains three cards from each region.
  4. Row and column status boxes stay blank until full, then show a green check for a balanced distribution or a red X for an incorrect one.
  5. Study mode shows or hides the region colors. Hint randomly places a card-bank item correctly before correcting a card already on the board.

Original activity: Adapted from Dunne, Paterson, and Spear, "GrID (GastroIntestinal Disease): An interactive Sudoku-based clinical anatomy card game for first-year medical students," Anatomical Sciences Education, 2026.
