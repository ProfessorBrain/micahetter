About

Created by Micah Etter, MD

Version 0.3

The EEG Machine is built as an educational tool for learning EEG patterns, artifacts, sleep stages, and seizure morphology in an interactive way.

It is meant to support curiosity, pattern recognition, and deliberate practice rather than replace clinical judgment.

Explore, compare patterns, test yourself, share your favorite EEG seed, and have fun!
