How to play

  1. Read the activity and its focus cue. Each cue has one best cranial nerve.
  2. Drag a nerve from the Nerve bank into the Best nerve slot, or tap it to place it.
  3. Match the supporting function. Bonus rounds also require one exact anatomy detail.
  4. Select Check answer. Every slot is compared directly with the stored solution.

Scoring

An exact nerve-and-function match earns 1 point. Foramina Fumble and Synaptic Snag add 1 point; Pathway Peril adds 2 points for an exact detail match.
