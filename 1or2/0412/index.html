What is shaping this

    About this quiz

    A simple question-based way to reflect on specialty fit.

    This is meant for first- and second-year medical students who want a rough starting point before clinical experience begins to narrow the field.

    How it works

    There are 102 A-or-B questions. Each answer adjusts the running ranking based on continuity, pace, procedures, uncertainty, population, and work setting.

    What you see

    The specialty ranking updates as you answer more questions. You can stop early for a rough read or continue for a more stable one.

    What it looks at

    What this quiz is measuring

    • Patient continuity versus episodic care
    • Procedures versus longitudinal management
    • Acute decision-making versus controlled pace
    • Breadth versus narrow domain focus
    • Diagnostic intensity, teamwork, and schedule shape

    Best Use

    Close results are common. They usually suggest a broader family of specialties rather than one clearly preferred field.

    Included Fields
