How to contribute

The library is maintained in a shared spreadsheet. Updates to that spreadsheet appear in the Hub automatically.

What to submit

  • Videos, lectures, slide decks, infographics, tweetorials, blogs, podcasts, and other durable teaching materials.
  • Include a stable URL and a short description.
  • If possible, tag with topic(s), learner level, and format.

Submission flow

  1. Collect the resource URL, title, creator/source, and a 1–2 sentence description.
  2. Provide suggested tags (topics, level, format) to help indexing.
  3. Send via your preferred internal workflow (spreadsheet edit or form).
