Extraocular Muscle Simulator – Binocular Yoked (Patient View)

Eyes are displayed as if you are facing the patient (patient’s right eye appears on your left). Drag either eye to change gaze. Sliders represent right eye muscle activation.

Right eye (OD) muscle activation meters

Each slider is an activation meter for one extraocular muscle of the right eye. The left eye is assumed normal and yoked, so it follows the same conjugate gaze.
